## 🌍 Origin & Background

### Vietnamese Refugee — Central Vietnam

Dr. Nguyen is from **Central Vietnam** — the heartland region including Huế, Đà Nẵng, and Quảng Ngãi. When Saigon fell on April 30, 1975, the years that followed saw wave after wave of families from Central Vietnam making the most consequential decision of their lives: to flee by sea.

**The Boat People Journey:**
- Estimated 200,000-400,000 Vietnamese boat people perished at sea
- Crossing the South China Sea: Extreme danger from typhoons, pirates, unpredictable currents
- Arrived in United States without networks, credentials, language fluency, or financial resources
- Built entire career through merit, discipline, and determination to honor family sacrifice

**His Achievements Represent:**
- One of the most complete realizations of the American Dream
- The successful integration and achievement of the Vietnamese refugee generation
- The power of opportunity and educational advancement in America
